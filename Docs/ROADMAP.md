### Week 5

- Ship apps list and uninstall preview flow

### Week 8

### Week 9

### Week 10

- Run quality, regression, and performance hardening

### Week 11

- Produce beta candidate and packaging pipeline

### Week 12

- Internal beta wrap-up and release-readiness review
